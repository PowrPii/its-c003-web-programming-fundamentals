Definition List

Soccer
             A game played on a field between two teams of 11 players each with the object to propel a round ball into the opponent's goal by kicking.
Basketball
             Two teams of five players each try to score by shooting a ball through a hoop elevated 10 feet above the ground.
Tennis
             A racket sport that can be played individually against a single opponent (singles) or between two teams of two players each (doubles).
Rugby
             A game played by two teams using an oval ball. Players try to score points by carrying the ball to their opponents' end of the field, or by kicking it over a bar fixed between two posts.
